At-Bat Baseball

A fully functional baseball simulation that utilizes 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, and 20 sided dice to determine the outcome of at-bats. Pick a team and hit "Play Ball!" to see if you win! Probabilities loosely based on 2016 MLB game averages (batting average, on base percentage, etc).

  • 12-sided die determines whether an at-bat results in a hit, walk, or out.
  • 20-sided die determines whether a hit is a single, double, triple, or homerun.
  • 4-sided die determines the type of out if there are no baserunners: strikeout, ground out, fly out, and line out.
  • 6-sided die determines the type of out if there is one baserunner: strikeout, ground out, fly out, line out, and double play.
  • 8-sided die determines the type of if there are two baserunners: strikeout, ground out, fly out, line out, and double play.
  • 10-sided die determines the type of out if the bases are loaded: strikeout, ground out, fly out, line out, double play, and triple play.

I'm working on the button functionality...so you can't actually "roll" the dice by pushing the buttons. :(

Open the console to see the roll-by-roll outcome updates for hit/out type, baserunners, and runs scored for each at-bat. There is also a score update at the end of each half-inning.
